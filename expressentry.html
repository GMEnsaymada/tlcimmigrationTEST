Express Entry

Express Entry is a pathway for skilled workers in Canada or overseas who want to immigrate to Canada permanently. This program selects skilled workers based on their skills and ability to contribute to Canada’s economy. This program manages three different federal economic immigration categories.

  • Federal Skilled Worker Program

  • Federal Skilled Trades Program

  • anadian Experience Class

How to Prepare for Express Entry

Language Test Results

To be eligible for this program, you must have a minimum CLB 7 (English) or NCLC 7 (French) for TEER 0 or TEER 1 occupations; and CLB 5(English) or NCLC 5 (French) for TEER 2 or TEER 3 occupations for all four language abilities (writing, reading, listening, speaking)..

Work Experience

You must have at least 1 year or 1,560 hours of skilled work experience in Canada in the last 3 years (either combination of full-time or part-time work). And must have gained your work experience by working in Canada while under temporary resident status with authorization to work..

The type or level of Canadian work experience in an occupation listed in 1 or more of NOC TEER 0-3 Categories. And must have performed the duties outlined in the lead statement of the occupational description in the NOC.

Education

There is no education requirement in this program. However, if you have Canadian or Foreign education. It will help improve your rank in the Express Entry pool. For Foreign education, send your credentials to Educational Credential Assessment (ECA) for Canadian equivalence assessment.
