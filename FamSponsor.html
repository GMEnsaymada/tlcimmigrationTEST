Family Sponsorship

Family Sponsorship allows Canadian citizens and Permanent Residents to sponsor their family members to immigrate permanently to Canada. You are eligible to sponsor if you are at least 18 years old, a Canadian citizen or permanent resident, financially capable of supporting your sponsored family member and not receiving social assistance except for disability. The family member you can sponsor includes your spouse, common-law partner, conjugal partner and dependent children. You can sponsor your adopted child and relatives in very specific situations, like an orphaned brother, sister, aunt or uncle. And other relatives if you don’t have a living family to sponsor.
